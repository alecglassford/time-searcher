Alec's Time Searcher: Stock prices over time

View this visualization with an extra chart zoomed in on selected stocks (warning: slower).

Looking for a particular stock? Search its ticker symbol here:

  • Drag boxes onto the grey canvas to filter stocks. Timeboxes select lines that are entirely within the box from left to right.
  • Click near the middle of a timebox and drag to move it around.
  • Click near the corner of a timebox and drag to resize it. The data is filtered discretely by weekly price, so if a box's width becomes thinner than 1 week, it will not filter at all and disappear once you stop dragging.
  • Rolling over lines to view individual ticker symbols works better on the zoomed in chart since there's less interference from other lines.
